October 20, 2015


A message from Fr. Seraphim Dedes

Dear DCS USERS: Glory to God for all things!
And Thank you. Thank you. Thank you.

Yes, thanks to you and your generous support over the past year, AGES Initiatives was able to maintain its public service of providing liturgical texts and music online for use in our churches. Not only were we able to maintain our offerings, but we added several enhancements and new functionalities as well.

In the coming year we plan to implement a login feature that will allow DCS users to set personal preferences. We are also developing a service book app for use by worshipers in the pews. And we will roll out the Translation Management System, which will facilitate the maintenance of liturgical libraries and the creation of new ones. We will also begin accepting parish clients for customized and expanded liturgical support; but more on that another time.

This is the time of year that we make our appeal for your continued financial support. AGES has not received financial support from any archdiocese or grant organization. We depend on donations from good people who value the free services we provide to churches throughout the world.

Once again, we are asking parishes to consider giving $250, and individual users to consider giving $120. Your financial support is needed to maintain and expand our programs to provide more and better liturgical texts and music.
